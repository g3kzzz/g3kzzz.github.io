About Me

G3Kzzz

Cybersecurity Enthusiast & CTF Player

Hello! I'm G3Kzzz, a passionate offensive security learner. I enjoy solving challenges, exploring vulnerabilities, and sharing detailed writeups to help others level up in cybersecurity.

My main focus is penetration testing, vulnerability research, and CTF competitions. I use platforms like HackTheBox, VulnHub, and TryHackMe to sharpen my skills.

Beyond hacking labs, I love mentoring and making complex technical concepts accessible to learners in the community. This project is my way of sharing knowledge and inspiring others in the infosec path.
