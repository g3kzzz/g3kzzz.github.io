About Me

Hello! I'm G3Kzzz, a cybersecurity enthusiast and CTF player. I enjoy solving challenges, exploring vulnerabilities, and documenting my learning process through detailed writeups. My main focus is offensive security, and I use platforms like HackTheBox and VulnHub to sharpen my skills.

Beyond hacking labs, I’m passionate about sharing knowledge and making technical concepts accessible for learners in the community. This project is part of that mission — to inspire and guide others in the infosec path.
